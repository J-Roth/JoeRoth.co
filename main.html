Case Study

Radar Relay

August 2017 - December 2018

I was co-founder and Marketing Director at Radar Relay, a blockchain based peer-to-peer application. I was in charge of our marketing efforts running solo initially and managing a team later on. Over a year and a half I mainly focused on our social channels, processes, analytics, press, educational websites, and google ads.

Social

Established various social channels. Included creation, maintaining brand consistency, and growth.

Twitter

18,500 Followers

Email

8,000 Signups

Blog

3,000 Readers

Process

Created a marketing brief process that accelerated the amount of content produced, developed and implemented a modified form of Agile to run weekly marketing sprints, and developed cross-team content processes.

Analytics

Defined how we created google events and implemented them to develop product and marketing dashboards. Using this data I was able to identify our most effective marketing strategies, inform product changes, and more.

Press

Worked with journalists to get Radar featured in publications such as Bloomberg, TechCrunch, Wall Street Journal, and the Denver Business Journal.

Educational Websites

Co-wrote, edit, and marketed multiple educational websites that explained complex topics. wETH, Relayer Network, Gwei, Wallet2Wallet, and more.

Google Ads

Ran an ad campaign for an educational site. It had a CTR of 8%, CPC of $0.38 and increased users on the site by over 300%.
