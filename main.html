Case Study

Radar Relay

August 2017 - December 2018

I was co-founder and Marketing Director at Radar Relay, a blockchain based peer-to-peer application. I was in charge of our digital marketing efforts running solo initially and managing a team later on. Over a year and a half I mainly focused on our analytics, SEO, processes, educational websites, social channels, and press.

Analytics

Implemented google events to look through hundreds of thousands of user actions and find the ones that mattered. With this data, I was able to identify our most effective marketing strategies, inform product changes, and more.

SEO

Conducted competitive analyses and studied target consumer behavior to identify the most effective keywords to focus on. Presented product briefs to improve site performance, indexability, and accessibility. Ran Google Ad campaigns on target keywords.

Process

Implemented a modified form of Scrum to run weekly point based marketing sprints. Developed cross-team content processes that were implemented company wide to facilitate cross functional collaboration. Team work makes the dream work.

Educational Websites

Co-wrote, edit, and marketed multiple educational websites that explained complex blockchain topics. wETH, Relayer Network, Gwei, Wallet2Wallet, and more.

Social

Established various social channels. Included creation, maintaining brand consistency, and growth.
